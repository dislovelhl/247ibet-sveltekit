# Storyboard

**Format:** 1920x1080
**Audio:** Kokoro voiceover + subtle electronic casino underscore + light UI SFX
**VO direction:** Calm, confident Canadian sportsbook register. Premium but direct; leave short pauses after the hook and before the CTA.
**Style basis:** DESIGN.md exact colors, fonts, components, and do/don't rules.

## Global Direction

This is a 30-second landscape promo built from the captured 247iBET homepage. Every beat lives on a deep navy canvas with visible gold energy: glints, outlines, number pulses, moving card stacks, and cinematic image motion. The logo appears in the opening and closing beats, the hero casino imagery anchors the signature visual, and responsible-play language stays visible whenever the video pushes action.

## BEAT 1 - COLD OPEN (0.00-3.05s)

**VO cue:** "Canada's betting night moves fast."

**Concept:** The viewer drops into a dark, premium casino floor at night. The gold 247iBET mark and the hero chip emerge through a slow push-in, while the phrase "moves fast" lands as a gold kinetic flash.

**Visual description:** Full-frame `casino-premium-hero.png` fills the background with a navy overlay. A large 247iBET logo floats near the upper left, the hero headline reconstructs in three staggered lines, a responsible-play pill glows in the upper right, and small gold line accents sweep across the roulette table. The camera subtly pushes toward the chip and cards while the word "FAST" expands in gold.

**Mood direction:** Premium sportsbook title sequence, not a generic casino ad. Dark, glossy, measured, and sharply lit.

**Assets:** `capture/assets/site/casino-premium-hero.png`, `capture/assets/site/logo.png`, `capture/screenshots/scroll-000.png`.

**Animation choreography:** Background slowly zooms; logo slides in with a short metallic glint; headline words rise and lock; the gold "FAST" scale-punches once; a thin SVG path draws around the CTA silhouette; responsible-play pill pulses gently.

**Transition:** Cinematic zoom through into Beat 2, 0.45s, blur clear on entry.

**Depth layers:** BG hero casino image; MG headline and logo; FG gold sweep lines and responsible-play pill.

**SFX cues:** Low warm riser at frame one. Soft gold whoosh on "fast."

## BEAT 2 - ONE PREMIUM ACCOUNT (3.05-10.10s)

**VO cue:** "247iBET brings casino, sportsbook, and fast payouts into one premium account."

**Concept:** The homepage becomes a premium command center. Casino, sportsbook, and payout proof are not separate promises; they fold into one account panel.

**Visual description:** A central dark glass panel shows "247iBET" and "One premium account." Three gold-edged modules orbit and settle: Casino, Sportsbook, Fast Payouts. Behind them, `scroll-023.png` pans slowly as a soft oversized proof layer. The rating, green check bullets, and comparison-table details are abstracted as animated microcards.

**Mood direction:** Executive dashboard crossed with iGaming lounge. Ordered, trustworthy, high conversion.

**Assets:** `capture/screenshots/scroll-023.png`, `capture/assets/site/logo.png`.

**Animation choreography:** Screenshot panel glides right; three modules cascade in with different y offsets; check marks draw as SVG strokes; a 4.8 rating counter ticks upward; gold border draws around the central account card.

**Transition:** Velocity-matched upward into Beat 3, 0.35s, with the module grid becoming product cards.

**Depth layers:** BG screenshot texture; MG central account card; FG orbiting feature chips and rating stars.

**SFX cues:** Three soft ticks as the modules lock.

## BEAT 3 - SCALE OF PLAY (10.10-14.90s)

**VO cue:** "Play more than one thousand casino games, bet more than thirty sports,"

**Concept:** The video opens into a dense wall of action. Casino and sportsbook cards slide into an eight-card premium grid while big numbers count up over them.

**Visual description:** Seven image cards form a wide cinematic carousel: slots, live casino, blackjack, sports betting, live betting, NHL, NBA. A huge "1000+ Casino Games" number locks on the left and "30+ Sports Covered" locks on the right. Tiny gold odds ticks and chips drift in the foreground, creating a live-market feel without clutter.

**Mood direction:** High-energy but controlled. Product breadth should feel abundant, not chaotic.

**Assets:** `capture/assets/site/casino-slots-hero.png`, `capture/assets/site/casino-live-hero.png`, `capture/assets/site/casino-blackjack-hero.png`, `capture/assets/site/sportsbook-premium-hero.png`, `capture/assets/site/sportsbook-live-betting-hero.png`, `capture/assets/site/sportsbook-nhl-hero.png`, `capture/assets/site/sportsbook-nba-hero.png`.

**Animation choreography:** Cards fan in from alternating directions; images use slow Ken Burns zooms; counters animate from zero; odds ticks type on in JetBrains Mono; gold corner brackets draw around the grid.

**Transition:** Blur-through to Beat 4, 0.4s.

**Depth layers:** BG navy radial glow; MG product card grid; FG counters, odds ticks, and gold brackets.

**SFX cues:** Light card shuffles under the counter rise.

## BEAT 4 - PAYOUT CONFIDENCE (14.90-24.80s)

**VO cue:** "and withdraw in as little as two to fifteen minutes. With twenty four seven support, bank-grade security, and responsible-play tools built in,"

**Concept:** Speed becomes proof. The four-step payout path draws like a secure rail from Deposit to Enjoy while trust badges light up around it.

**Visual description:** Four large step cards fill the frame: Deposit, Play, Withdraw, Enjoy. A bright gold line connects them, and green timing pills pop on "Instant," "2-15 mins," and "Up to 15 mins." To the right, support, security, and responsible-play badges stack vertically with restrained green highlights. `fast-payouts-hero.png` sits as a tilted background card.

**Mood direction:** Financial trust meets iGaming speed. Clean, secure, fast.

**Assets:** `capture/assets/site/fast-payouts-hero.png`, `capture/screenshots/scroll-046.png`.

**Animation choreography:** The rail draws left to right; cards rise in sequence; timing pills pop with a tiny glow; shield/headset icons trace on; the background payout image slowly tilts and breathes.

**Transition:** Whip pan into Beat 5, 0.32s, following the gold rail.

**Depth layers:** BG screenshot/image slab; MG step cards; FG rail, pills, and trust badges.

**SFX cues:** Soft payment confirmation chime on "two to fifteen minutes."

## BEAT 5 - CTA CLOSE (24.80-32.20s)

**VO cue:** "it's designed for Canadian players who want confidence before the first click. Join 247iBET now."

**Concept:** The video resolves into the final CTA band. The brand stops moving just enough to feel reliable, then the gold button invites action.

**Visual description:** A wide final card echoes the homepage CTA: "Ready to Open Your 247iBET Account?" The 247iBET logo sits above the headline, a small "19+ Play Responsibly" pill stays visible, and the gold button brightens on the final line. The footer screenshot adds dark grid depth behind the final CTA without becoming readable clutter.

**Mood direction:** Premium close, direct response, responsible and confident.

**Assets:** `capture/assets/site/logo.png`, `capture/screenshots/scroll-100.png`.

**Animation choreography:** Footer screenshot drifts downward; logo fades and sharpens; headline builds word by word; responsible-play pill pulses once; CTA button glows and arrow slides right; final frame holds for inspection.

**Transition:** Final scene fades to deep navy after the CTA hold.

**Depth layers:** BG footer screenshot/grid; MG final CTA band; FG logo, responsible-play pill, and button glow.

**SFX cues:** Clean final chime on "now"; underscore resolves.
