# Design System

## Overview

247iBET presents itself as a premium Canadian casino and sportsbook destination. The site uses a dark, glossy navy shell with oversized white display typography, prestige-gold calls to action, and cinematic casino/sports imagery. Layouts are dense but orderly: hero split composition, feature strips, comparison tables, payout-step cards, category cards, FAQ rows, and a final CTA band. The mood is high-trust iGaming: dramatic, polished, fast, and explicitly responsible-play oriented.

## Colors

- **Primary Surface**: `#070C18` - near-black navy background for the page and video base.
- **Card Surface**: `#0D1629` - main card and panel fill.
- **Raised Surface**: `#142038` - elevated modules, table headers, and internal card accents.
- **Primary Content**: `#FFFFFF` - major headlines and high-contrast labels.
- **Muted Content**: `#94A3B8` - body copy and secondary UI text.
- **Secondary Muted Content**: `#6A7282` - low-emphasis footer and metadata.
- **Prestige Gold**: `#D4943A` - CTAs, key words, icons, outlines, and positive highlights.
- **Bright Gold**: `#EBB55E` - glints, strokes, and small animated accents.
- **Deep Gold**: `#B87826` - CTA depth and shadowed gold gradients.
- **Success Green**: `#22C55E` - responsible-play status, payout speed, and trust signals.

## Typography

- **Display/Sans**: Plus Jakarta Sans (400, 500, 600, 700, 900). Used for large headers, CTA text, cards, and most interface copy.
- **Body**: Be Vietnam Pro (300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 900). Used for readable supporting copy and compact informational sections.
- **Mono**: JetBrains Mono (400). Used sparingly for odds, timings, labels, payout speeds, and responsibility microcopy.
- **Hierarchy**: Hero display reaches about 102px at desktop, section headings around 30-48px, card titles around 16px, and labels use high tracking with all-caps treatment.

## Elevation

Depth comes from layered navy panels, thin borders, inner highlights, and warm gold glow rather than conventional shadows. CTA buttons use a gold gradient with inset white highlight and a soft amber drop shadow. Cards rely on one-pixel translucent borders, rounded 12-16px corners, and subtle surface shifts from `#070C18` to `#0D1629` and `#142038`. Cinematic images are darkened with navy overlays so gold details and white type stay readable.

## Components

- **Cinematic Hero**: Large left-aligned headline with gold emphasis, dark casino image background, and a hero chip/card composition on the right.
- **Responsible Play Status Pill**: Small green-dot pill, high tracking, compact and persistent in the nav.
- **Gold Primary CTA**: Rounded pill button with amber gradient, black text, arrow icon, and soft glow.
- **Payout Feature Strip**: Six horizontally aligned trust cells with gold line icons, title, and muted subtitle.
- **Recommended Platform Panel**: Two-column dark card with rating stars, green check bullets, and comparison table.
- **Payout Step Cards**: Four numbered blocks with gold circular step markers and green timing pills.
- **Casino/Sports Category Grid**: Dark image cards using gold borders and small action labels.
- **Guide/FAQ Rows**: Compact editorial cards and accordion rows with restrained borders.
- **Final CTA Band**: Wide dark panel with gold outline, large headline, and right-aligned gold CTA.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's

- Use `#070C18`, `#0D1629`, and `#142038` as the core surface stack.
- Keep gold accents concentrated on CTAs, icons, data highlights, and kinetic glints.
- Use large, confident white type with a single gold phrase or number for emphasis.
- Keep imagery cinematic and darkened enough for text readability.
- Include responsible-play and Canada-specific trust signals when the scene claims credibility.

### Don'ts

- Do not use bright full-screen color backgrounds; the experience should stay navy and premium.
- Do not flatten the visuals into text-only slides; use images, chips, tables, and card stacks.
- Do not use playful casino clutter or cartoon motifs; this brand is polished and restrained.
- Do not obscure required 19+ and responsible-play signals when referencing play.
- Do not use generic blue/purple SaaS gradients; gold on navy is the signature.
